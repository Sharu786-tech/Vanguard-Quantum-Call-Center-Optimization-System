# Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Title
Vanguard Call Center Quantum Optimization

## Slide 2: Business Problem
Call volume varies by hour, market behavior, and client needs. Staffing must balance cost
and service quality.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution
Hybrid forecasting, QUBO optimization, QAOA extension, and simulation.

## Slide 4: Architecture
Data -> Forecasting -> QUBO Builder -> Solver -> Simulation -> Dashboard

## Slide 5: Optimization Model
Minimize staffing cost plus penalties for under-service and overstaffing.

## Slide 6: Quantum Fit
Binary decision variables and many possible schedules make this a good QUBO candidate.

## Slide 7: Prototype Results
Show forecast volume, planned agents, service-level target hit rate, and wait time.

## Slide 8: Roadmap
Pilot, quantum backend integration, multi-skill scheduling, production dashboard.
